Detailed Next Steps Guide

This guide expands the landing page steps and walks you through each action with tips and links.

1

Create an account

Register with your email to access your dashboard and profile. No social logins required.

  • Use a valid email; verification links expire after 24 hours.
  • After confirming, you'll be redirected to login.
Go to Login / Register
2

Fill your profile

Add your birth details to enable resonance-based matching. Approximate birth time is fine; more accurate improves results.

  • Enter birth date, time, and place. If you don't know the exact time, pick an estimate.
  • Write a short intro that reflects your interests and intentions. Be specific and genuine.
Tip: Your profile timezone is detected automatically; you can review it in your dashboard.
Edit Profile
3

Create a time slot

Offer one or more time slots when you'd be open to connect. We store your local time and convert to UTC for scheduling.

  • Pick a start time in your local timezone; choose a duration (e.g., 1–3 hours).
  • Adding slots on multiple days increases the chance of quick matches.
Timezone aware: We handle conversions between your local time and UTC automatically (UTC±HH:MM).
Open Dashboard
4

Watch out for matches

Browse suggested matches based on resonance. Check compatibility notes and shared interests.

  • New suggestions appear as your profile and slots are available.
  • Use filters and notes to track who you'd like to contact.
Privacy first: Your details remain private. You choose when and how to engage.
5

Propose a meetup or confirm

When interest is mutual, propose a time within overlapping slots or accept a proposal from your match.

  • Send a friendly, specific invitation. Suggest one or two time windows.
  • Confirm the plan in chat, then move to a call when both are ready.
Consent and comfort: move at your own pace. It's okay to keep it text-only.
6

Join the Jitsi chat

Use Jitsi for optional voice/video. Desktop browsers (Firefox, Chrome) are supported. Text chat is always available.

  • Test mic/camera, or keep them off and use text only.
  • Pick the right devices via the small arrow next to mic/cam icons.
Read the Jitsi quick-start

Enjoy connecting in a way that feels right for you.
